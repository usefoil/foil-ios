# Foil iOS v0.23 Beta Recovery UX

## Outcome

Handle beta-blocking recovery and UX friction discovered by the physical
rehearsal without turning this conveyor into broad feature work.

## Oracle

This board is complete only when every discovered beta-blocking issue from the
preflight, TestFlight rehearsal, and Apple app matrix is either fixed and proven,
externally blocked with exact operator action, or explicitly deferred as
acceptable closed-beta risk.

## Non-Goals

- Do not add third-party app support.
- Do not redesign the app broadly.
- Do not implement speculative polish without physical evidence.

`/goal Follow docs/goals/ios-v0.23-beta-recovery-ux/goal.md.`
