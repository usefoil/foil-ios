# Foil iOS v0.31 Closed Beta Tester Handoff

## Outcome

Create the tester-facing handoff for the narrow internal TestFlight beta:
install/setup steps, known caveats, safe test tasks, and a privacy-preserving
feedback template.

## Oracle

The board is complete when a fresh build-11 tester handoff exists, stale v0.7
copy is retired or clearly superseded, and scans prove the handoff does not
overclaim Mail, Messages delivery, private-thread behavior, or arbitrary app
support.
