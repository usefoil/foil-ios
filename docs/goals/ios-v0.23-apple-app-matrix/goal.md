# Foil iOS v0.23 Apple App Matrix

## Outcome

Physically prove Foil Keyboard insertion across the closed-beta Apple app
baseline: Notes, Messages/iMessage, Mail, and Safari/text fields.

## Oracle

This board is complete only when every row has sanitized before/action/after
evidence, exact-once insertion or honest blocker state, and App Group cleanup
evidence. Secure/private fields must be recorded as expected limitations, not
failures disguised as passes.

## Non-Goals

- Do not test third-party apps.
- Do not inspect private conversations, emails, notes, browsing content, or
  account data.
- Do not send real messages or emails unless the operator explicitly approves a
  sterile self/test target.
