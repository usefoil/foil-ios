# Foil iOS v0.42 First-Run Onboarding Polish

## Outcome

Make the Foil iOS first-run and returning-user setup experience closed-beta
ready: testers should know what Foil does, what setup remains, how to enable
the keyboard, where it is safe to test, and how to recover from common setup
failures without relying on external docs.

## Oracle

This board is complete when simulator and physical iPhone receipts prove the
first-run, partially configured, keyboard-blocked, and setup-complete states are
visible, actionable, and accurately scoped for closed beta. Copy scans must show
no broad iPhone app, Mail, Messages delivery, secure-field, or public App Store
claims.

`/goal Follow docs/goals/ios-v0.42-first-run-onboarding-polish/goal.md.`
