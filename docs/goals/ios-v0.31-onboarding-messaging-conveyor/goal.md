# Foil iOS v0.31 Onboarding Messaging Conveyor

## Original Request

Plan the next onboarding, tester handoff, and public messaging work as a
GoalBuddy board conveyor.

## Outcome

Create a sequenced set of execution-ready boards that turns the v0.30 narrow
closed-beta go decision into coherent tester instructions, polished iOS app
onboarding, landing-page messaging, and a final copy/proof audit.

## Oracle

The conveyor is complete when every child board is either complete with receipts
or precisely blocked, and the final audit proves public/user-facing copy does
not overclaim Mail, arbitrary app support, Messages delivery, or private-thread
behavior.

## Child Boards

1. `docs/goals/ios-v0.31-closed-beta-tester-handoff/goal.md`
2. `docs/goals/ios-v0.32-ios-app-onboarding-polish/goal.md`
3. `docs/goals/ios-v0.33-foil-landing-ios-preview-messaging/goal.md`
4. `docs/goals/ios-v0.34-onboarding-messaging-final-audit/goal.md`

## Non-Goals

- Do not upload or install a new TestFlight build in this conveyor unless a
  child board explicitly approves that later.
- Do not broaden product claims beyond the v0.30 readiness boundary.
- Do not inspect private phone content, Mail inboxes, Messages threads, or
  tester feedback containing private data.

## Starter Command

`/goal Follow docs/goals/ios-v0.31-onboarding-messaging-conveyor/goal.md.`
